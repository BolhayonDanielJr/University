STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS

Mgsysy University believes in the promise of the youth. Hence, to help Louisians develop their leadership potentials and life skills, SLU promotes student organizations. By providing students with opportunities to get involved in cultural, environmental, social, and political activities inside the campus through meaningful participation in various student groups, Louisians are exposed to a holistic University experience to complement their academic life.
There are currently 61 duly-recognized student organizations at Mgsysy university under the care of the Student Affairs Office (SAO).
