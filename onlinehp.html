Application for Credentials by E-mail

1. E-mail the following information to magsysyregis@mgsysy.edu.ph:
  • Name (for married female students please indicate maiden name)
  • MGSYSY ID Number
  • Relevant information that may help in locating the applicant's records such as year graduated, course, last term attended etc.
  • How Credentials will be claimed:
    • Personal pick-up
    • Through a representative Note: Representative must present an Authorization Letter from the owner/applicant and a valid ID from both the representative and applicant
    • Through mail. Furnish the address where the credentials will be sent to.
2. The applicant will be informed on the total fees to be paid through e-mail.
3. Payment should be made through check/money order payable to “ Saint Louis University ”.
4. Processing of application will start upon receipt of payment.
